Leadership

I lead with visibility, data & ownership

…and build those same qualities into my team.

📣 Communicate up, consistently

Structured weekly updates to leadership — initiative status, contributor-level data, blockers, next steps — making the team’s work legible without being asked.

🤝 Delegate ownership, not tasks

Assign end-to-end ownership of major initiatives and credit each person to leadership, building their visibility and growth.

🎯 Coach before the moment

Prep team members with structured talking points before key meetings — developing presence and confidence.

📈 Make performance visible

Track contribution trends over 17 months to prove impact with numbers, not assertions.

🔄 Pivot fast on bad bets

Diagnose root cause, document what works, and propose a better workflow instead of pushing a failing path.

🌱 Define growth paths

Five growth axes for the team: analytics & strategy, AI & automation, technical enablement, documentation architecture, and mentorship.

🛡️ Advocate for the team

Escalate real workflow friction to tooling/IT leadership with a clear business case — advocating for conditions, not just output.

🔬 Engineering-level rigor

Run formal RCAs on rejected contributions to fix upstream quality and reduce wasted effort.
